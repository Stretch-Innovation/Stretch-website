Trade fairs are one of the most expensive line items in a B2B marketing budget, and one of the least measured.

## Before the event

Pre-event outreach to your target account list, landing pages with clear value propositions, and meeting scheduling automation turn passive booth traffic into qualified conversations.

## During and after

Real-time lead capture into your CRM, same-day follow-up sequences, and lead scoring ensure hot prospects don't go cold while your team travels home.

## The ROI framework

Measure cost per qualified lead, meeting-to-opportunity rate, and pipeline generated, not just foot traffic and badge scans.
