Food companies don't lack ambition. They lack bandwidth.

Between production schedules, distributor relationships, and regulatory compliance, growth marketing often gets relegated to trade shows and occasional campaigns. The result: unpredictable pipeline and no first-party data.

## The shift we're seeing

Leading food companies are building direct B2B relationships alongside traditional distribution — with gated content, lead scoring, and automated nurture sequences that run without constant manual effort.

## Proof it works

From Vandemoortele's B2B engine to brewery D2C launches, the pattern is the same: start small, prove the model, then scale systematically.
